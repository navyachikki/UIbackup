CSS Padding & Margin prop 
    margin: The gap/space been occupied above the border of the element is called margin space 

    margin: 10px; to apply 10 px of margin for all the four directions 

    margin-top: 10px 
    margin-left 
    margin-bottom
    margin-right 

    margin: 10px 20px; 10px will be for both top and bottom, 20px for left and right 

    margin: 5px 30px 20px 19px; (top, right, bottom, left)

    Padding: The space been occuped below the border and above the text content. 

    CSS Box model:  
        the concept of calculating the actual dimensions of DOM element on the page, is called css box modeling

        While calculating the actual dimensions, it includes, the border, margin space, padding space, and the given dimentions
