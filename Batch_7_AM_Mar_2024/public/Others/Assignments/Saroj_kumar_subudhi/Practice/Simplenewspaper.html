News   item  1-:

In the first two decades of the 21st century, the world had to press reboot button twice—during the financial crisis in 2008 and when a microscopic virus snapped lives out of millions between 2019 and 2021. Both disasters changed the world order forever, but one country emerged stronger every time—India, that is Bharat.
The post pandemic world is caught in two unending wars, one along Russia-Ukraine border and one in Gaza. These unwarranted conflicts, where human lives are daily casualties, now have disrupted the global sanity once again, throwing mankind into an uncertain future. The American supremacy is no longer what it used to be, the pace of China’s growth has slowed down, Russia is embroiled in a war it can’t get out of, Europe is caught in its own inertia, Africa is still struggling to play catch-up with the rest of the world and West Asian conflict threatens to spiral out of control any moment.
